Lasagna Recipe

Description

A delicious lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Ricotta
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Add sauce to the meat
  4. Add a layer of sauce to a tray
  5. Add a layer of noodles to the tray
  6. Add a layer of meat sauce to the tray
  7. Add a layer of noodles to the tray
  8. Add a layer of ricotta to the tray
  9. Add a layer of noodles to the tray
  10. Add a layer of sauce to the tray
  11. Consume
